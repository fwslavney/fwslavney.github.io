» Laboratory Midterm Examination -- Friday, April 9, 2021

This laboratory midterm will cover the first SIX labs that we have completed. Lab exams may include short answer and practical questions covering physiological principles, lab procedures, terminology, identification of structures, and explanation of their functions. Students should also keep in mind that material from lecture is fair grounds to be on a laboratory exam. For this examination, you should be able to . . .

  • Define science and understand the logic and sequence of the scientific method.
  • Develop productive observations, questions, and hypotheses about the natural world.
  • Calculate the range, mean, and standard deviation for a set of replicate measurements.
  • Design and conduct a controlled experiment to test a null hypothesis.
  • Understand the difference between a hypothesis and a scientific theory.
